ABOUT ME

"I am a woman, therefore I am satire." . . .
You are visiting the Sebring sARTorial Salon. Yes sartorial means tailored elegance - check out my trousers on the Stage Page! - but it looks and sounds of the root satura (satire) which it is not. But my word logic is satire + ART = sARTorial! Take it or leave it. With degrees in Literature, Film, Theater and Motion Graphics my canvas is the PAGE, the STAGE and the SCREEN. Animations selected M.A.A. and Spotty Dog International Film Festivals. Screenwriter/Playwright participant in Chatham Film Festival, UA Playwrights' Festival and New York Stage and Film Powerhouse Theatre. Winner inaugural New York Women in Film and TV Feature Screenplay Competition. Critically compared to John Waters, my cheesy works can hold it to the sagacious Shavian wit of my "serious" side. Please delight in and think about the animations and excerpts. You can contact me if you are interested in the plays or screenplays or whatever. Specialty: Dancer/Choreographer (stage and film), also Equestrian and Model (Industrial, fashion, life). Member DG and SAG/AFTRA, NYWIFT. Proud grad NYU Film School. Dual citizenship EU and American Passport holder.
